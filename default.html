About Scott

J. Scott Lopes is a long time Disney fan who first went to Walt Disney World as a child in 1989 and has enjoyed traveling to Orlando ever since. He is interested in all things Disney Parks-related, especially the Walt Disney Imagineering division and all of the work and detail that they put into everything that they engineer.
